CITY OF THE WORLD visualizing country wise population density change
City of the world is an interactive three dimensional data visualization exploring the variation in population density change across various countries. It utilizes a volumetric treemap structure inspired by the metaphor of high-rise buildings and underground dungeons in a city. This visualization aims at drawing attention to increase and decrease in population densities (number of people per unit area) in a country instead of looking at the overall population variation, telling a lot more about the demographics of a country.

-- ANALOGY --

city — world
buildings — increase in population of a country
underground dungeons — decrease in population
height of building — extent of rise of population
depth of building — extent of downfall of population
area of building — surface area of country
